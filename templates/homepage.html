Crop-Yield-Prediction

Crop yield prediction remains one of the most challenging tasks in agriculture as it affects decision making at global, regional, and field levels. The prediction of crop yield is based on several factors such as soil, meteorological, environmental, and crop parameters. Due to the colossal effect agriculture has on the economy of the country, data-driven prediction models need to be developed. Against this background, machine learning (ML) models were engineered after processing agricultural data and Regression models were deployed to predict the yield.

Dataset
